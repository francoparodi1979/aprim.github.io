## About the study

HELIX-3 evaluates an investigational oral antifibrotic, taken alongside
standard therapy, in adults living with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. The
study aims to measure changes in lung function (FVC) and patient-reported
breathing symptoms over approximately six months.

## Who can participate

You may be eligible if you:

- Are 50–85 years old
- Have a confirmed diagnosis of IPF
- Are on stable pirfenidone or nintedanib for at least 12 weeks
- Can attend nine on-site visits over 26 weeks

## What to expect

- Lung function testing (spirometry, DLCO) at each visit
- Investigational drug provided at no cost
- Compensation for time and travel
