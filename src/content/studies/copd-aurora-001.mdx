## About the study

AURORA-1 evaluates an investigational once-daily inhaled therapy designed to
improve lung function and reduce exacerbations in adults living with
moderate-to-severe COPD. Participants attend study visits at our Bloomfield
Hills office over approximately six months.

## Who can participate

You may be eligible if you:

- Are between 40 and 80 years old
- Have a diagnosis of moderate-to-severe COPD
- Have a smoking history of at least 10 pack-years
- Are willing to attend seven on-site visits over 24 weeks

A research coordinator will confirm eligibility at a screening visit.

## What to expect

- An initial screening visit including spirometry and basic labs
- Six follow-up visits with breathing tests and study medication review
- Compensation for time and travel at each visit
