## About the study

This Phase 2 dose finding study is evaluating GSK5784283, an investigational
anti-TSLP antibody, in adults aged 18 to 75 with uncontrolled asthma despite
standard inhaled therapy. This study is currently active, with no new
participants being enrolled.

## Who can participate

You may be eligible if you:

- Are 18–75 years old
- Have a diagnosis of asthma that remains uncontrolled on current therapy
- Are currently on a daily controller inhaler

This study is active but not currently recruiting. Contact us to be
considered for future studies in this program.

## What to expect

- Lung function testing and symptom assessments at each visit
- Investigational study drug provided at no cost
- Compensation for time and travel
