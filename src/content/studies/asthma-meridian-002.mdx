## About the study

MERIDIAN-2 is evaluating an investigational injectable biologic for adults
with severe asthma whose symptoms persist despite high-dose inhaled
corticosteroids. The study runs approximately one year with monthly visits.

## Who can participate

You may be eligible if you:

- Are 18–75 years old
- Have a diagnosis of asthma
- Are currently on a daily controller inhaler
- Have had at least one asthma flare in the last year requiring oral steroids

## What to expect

- Monthly study visits over 52 weeks
- Investigational study drug provided at no cost
- Compensation for time and travel
